Account & Data Deletion - IWAC Conference App

This page explains how users of the IWAC application can request deletion of their account and associated data.

How to Request Deletion

To delete your account and associated data, please send an email to:

iwac@wacassociation.org

You must send the request from the email address that is registered with your account.

Please include the following subject line: "DELETE ACCOUNT"

What Data Is Deleted

When you request account deletion, we will delete:

  • Your account profile information
  • Your login credentials
  • Any associated user-generated data within the app

Data Retention

Some data may be retained for a limited period for legal, security, or operational purposes. This retention period will not exceed a reasonable timeframe (typically up to 30 days), after which all remaining data is permanently removed.

Contact

If you have questions about data deletion, contact:
iwac@wacassociation.org
